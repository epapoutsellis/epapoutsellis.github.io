Hello, my name is Evangelos (or better Vaggelis) Papoutsellis and I am a Research Associate at the University of Manchester. I work in the Collaborative Computational Project in Tomographic Imaging, aka [CCPi](https://www.ccpi.ac.uk/) headed by Profs [Philip Withers]({{site.data.coauthors.Withers.url}}) and [William Lionheart]({{site.data.coauthors.Lionheart.url}}).

During January 2016 - October 2018, I was a Research Associate at the [Institut Denis Poisson](https://www.idpoisson.fr/), Université d'Orléans and at the Faculté de Médecine, Université de Tours. I was working with Prof. [Maïtine Bergounioux]({{site.data.coauthors.Bergounioux.url}}) and Prof. [Clovis Tauber]({{site.data.coauthors.Tauber.url}}) in dynamic PET reconstruction and with Prof. [Christophe Destrieux]({{site.data.coauthors.Destrieux.url}}) in validating in & ex vivo MRI brain registration.

Prior to that I completed my PhD under the supervision of Prof. [Carola-Bibiane Schönlieb]({{site.data.coauthors.Schonlieb.url}}) at the Cambridge Image Analysis group of Department of Applied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics at the University of Cambridge.
